2. Introduction

Figure 1: Conceptual Illustration of Experiential Alignment. The agent aligns its internal world model via Process Experience and Goal Experience.

Motivation

1
Addressing Physical Hallucination: LLMs possess vast semantic knowledge but lack procedural grounding in physical laws, resulting in plans that are logically sound but physically unexecutable.
2
Overcoming Traditional Fine-tuning Limitations: Alignment strategies relying on parameter fine-tuning are not only resource-intensive but also inherently rigid, making it difficult to adapt to dynamically changing physical environments.
3
Lacking Efficient Experiential Mechanisms: Existing methods lack an efficient training-free experiential learning mechanism to align the agent's internal world model with physical reality.
